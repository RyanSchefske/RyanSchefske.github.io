Get Up Instructions

Unfortunately, Get Up - Active Alarm Clock is at the mercy of Apple when it comes to approved functionality. Therefore, Get Up requires push notifications to be turned on for appropriate use. This also requires Do Not Disturb mode, or any equivalent focus mode, be turned off in order for Get Up to work properly and get you out of bed! Additionally, Get Up must be running in the background in order to work. So do not fully close out of the app!

Important Steps

  • Allow push notifications
  • Do not fully close out of the app
  • Turn off Do Not Disturb
  • Allow camera access
  • Open the app after it is updated, or changing time zones
